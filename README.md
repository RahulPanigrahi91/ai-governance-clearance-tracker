AI Governance Clearance Tracker is a front-end prototype that helps finance operations move AI prototypes to production with
better structure and faster review. It includes structured intake, transparent risk scoring, dynamic governance checklists, and
leadership visibility into bottlenecks.

This prototype demonstrates how governance enablement can work as an operating system: builders arrive review-ready the first time,
reviewers see pipeline health upstream, and recurring feedback becomes playbook improvements.

Core features:
- Dashboard with KPIs, stage distribution, risk mix, and time-to-clearance trend
- Solutions registry with searchable risk register
- New submission form with live risk scoring and checklist generation
- Checklist builder with readiness updates and text export
- Feedback analytics to reduce review loops
- Plain-language governance playbooks (DPDP, EU AI Act, SOX, Responsible AI)
