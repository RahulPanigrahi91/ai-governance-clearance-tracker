# AI Governance Clearance Tracker

AI Governance Clearance Tracker is a working front-end prototype that shows how an internal governance team can help builders move AI solutions from prototype to production faster, with better visibility and fewer compliance loops.

## One-line description

A governance enablement dashboard for AI intake, risk scoring, checklist generation, review tracking, and feedback-driven process improvement.

## What it demonstrates

- Structured intake for AI prototypes.
- Transparent risk scoring based on privacy, finance controls, human oversight, and deployment scope.
- Dynamic governance checklist generation.
- Leadership visibility into risk register health and review bottlenecks.
- Plain-language playbooks that translate policy into action.

## Why it matters

Most governance programs fail when they act like late-stage blockers. This prototype shows a better model: governance as an operating system that helps builders arrive review-ready the first time.

## Best use in an interview

Use the project to talk about:
